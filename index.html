What is Spilhuset?
Danish Join our space

The best place
to build things
in Copenhagen

Spilhuset is a magical place located in the heart of Copenhagen. Historically home to some of the nations best game developers, Spilhuset is the perfect environment for game and tech startups to meet, think, build and celebrate. Since the mid 2000s, talented and ambitious startups have contributed to our community’s culture, making it Denmark’s biggest cluster of game companies.

With the spirit of the community, we’re opening a cozy 620 m2 apartment-style coworking space. This new space offers the perfect compromise between intimate working space and tech community. From private space (desk or room) to conference rooms and common areas, we provide everything startups need to succeed.

 

What we offer

As a company or individual in Spilhuset you get access to a fully furnished office environment in lovely surroundings. Height adjustable tables and great office chairs. High speed internet access, regularly cleaned spaces and optional lunch arrangements. Everyone has access to both small and large meeting rooms, our big dining hall and several cozy nooks for co-working or relaxing. In other words, it’s the perfect place to work on your dream project.
